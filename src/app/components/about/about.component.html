Who are we?

Alexander – Fine Attire is made to measure manufacturer for men’s suits and shirts. The company was founded in 2011 and has since then grown its loyal customer base in and around Johannesburg. More than 600 customers make regular use of their digitally saved patterns, making it easy for them to shop their wardrobe essentials.

Our logo, the oaktree- crest, represents an old worker’s guild. Solid, reliable and consistent workmanship is our strength, natural materials and textures our handwriting. All of this resembled by our beautiful logo that is embroidered in each suit jacket.

We operate out of the refurbished Tattersalls Building in Johannesburg City. Wooden floors, secure parking and a fully private showroom create a unique atmosphere of shopping.
